Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Prepare the Dough

Take your pizza dough out of the refrigerator 30 minutes before use. Let it come to room temperature for easier stretching and shaping.

↓
2

Preheat Your Oven

Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven while preheating for optimal crispiness.

↓
3

Shape and Stretch

On a floured surface, gently stretch the dough into a 12-inch circle. Use your hands to create a slightly thicker edge for the crust.

↓
4

Add Toppings

Spread tomato sauce evenly, leaving a 1-inch border. Add mozzarella cheese and your favorite toppings. Don't overload to ensure even cooking.

↓
5

Bake to Perfection

Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly. Let cool for 2 minutes, slice, and enjoy your homemade pizza!
